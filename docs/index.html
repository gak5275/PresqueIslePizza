The West Millcreek Food Pantry gives out staple food items on each distribution day. We provide meat, dairy, fresh produce, and other items as available. We also provide the 60+ supplemental food boxes for our neighbors age 60 and older who qualify. We mainly serve the 16505 and 16506 area codes (Millfair Rd to Pittsburgh Ave / West 38th to West 12th).
